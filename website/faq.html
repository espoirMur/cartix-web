What is Cartix?

Cartix is a web platform that enables organizations to optimize operational performance, strategic investments,
and enhances everyday decision making processes through adequate, timely and advanced data exploitation.

It can also be referred to as a data management system with a mapping intelligent module that allows users
to visualize data in time and space as they monitor different dynamics as well as corresponding qualitative & quantitative analytics tailored to address specific objectives within an organization.

Who can use cartix?

Users of Cartix span from individuals to both small and large organizations (private & public) that want to make
sense of data they generate on a daily basis as well as data that’s publicly available.

Cartix allows you to have a better understanding of correlation and causal factors of a given phenomenon and
the contrast in between.

For now Cartix addresses both the descriptive and diagnostic aspects of data and soon it will be extensively addressing the predictive aspect to even give more control to the users
